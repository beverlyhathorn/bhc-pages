Common Questions

Will it sound robotic or fake?

No. The voice is conversational and professional — not a phone tree. Callers are greeted naturally, asked relevant questions, and handled with the same tone your office would use. Before going live, you listen to a full test call and approve how it sounds. Nothing goes active until you're satisfied.

What happens during setup?

Setup takes 3–5 business days. We collect your business hours, service area, call routing preferences, and urgency criteria. Then we build and test the system. On day four, you review a live test call and request any adjustments. On day five, you go live. No technical work is required on your end.

Can I cancel anytime?

After the initial 3-month term, yes — with 30 days written notice. The 3-month minimum exists to give the system enough time to be properly configured and deliver measurable results. After that, your continued engagement is your choice.

What if a caller has a complex issue the AI can't handle?

The system is configured with clear transfer and escalation rules. If a situation falls outside the intake script — or a caller requests to speak with a person — the call is transferred or flagged immediately. It's designed to know its limits.

Will it work with my existing phone number?

Yes. Your existing number stays in place. Calls ring to your office first. If unanswered, they route to the AI receptionist. There is no number change and no disruption to how your team currently operates.

How do I know it's working?

Every month you receive a performance summary showing total calls handled, urgency breakdown, average call duration, and key trends. Ongoing monitoring is included in your retainer — you're never left wondering what's happening in the background.

Who will manage it?

Beverly Hathorn manages your system directly. You are not handed off to a support team or a ticketing system. Configuration updates, script refinements, and monthly reviews are handled personally as part of your ongoing retainer.
